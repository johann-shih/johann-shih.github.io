What needs to be clarified is what type of astrology we are discussing.

Because astrology can be divided into broad-sense astrology or narrow-sense astrology.

广义的占星学主要的是说天象与预兆
Broad-sense astrology primarily refers to celestial phenomena and omens.

天空中出现的某种景象可能对应现实中发生了什么事项，这个其实就是对应着天象或预兆的概念
A certain phenomenon appearing in the sky might correspond to events happening in reality. This corresponds to the concept of celestial phenomena or omens.

当然它也包含各种衍生的概念，因为毕竟是广义上的占星学
Of course, it also includes various derived concepts. After all, it’s astrology in the broad sense.

And prediction methods derived from astronomical calendars can also be called astrology.

Since there are actually many such methods, I won’t go into detail here.

We are mainly discussing narrow-sense astrology.

当我们说「占星学」时，我们是在一个宏大的、更学术的背景下去讨论它
When we say “Zhànxīngxué” (Astrology as a Theory, Academic Study in Chinese), we are discussing it within a grand, more academic context.

「占星术」只是更侧重于作为一项工具的技术和应用
“Zhànxīngshù” focuses more on the technique and application as a tool.

所以，我们说的这个狭义的占星学，指的就是这个天宫图占星学
So, the narrow-sense astrology we refer to is this Horoscopic Astrology.

I hope you can understand the relationship between “Zhànxīngxué” and “Zhànxīngshù” in Chinese.

因为「学」的本身包含了「术」，比如说「医学」跟「手术」的这个关系
Because the “Academic Study” itself encompasses the “Technique”. For example, the relationship between “Medicine” and “Surgery”.

我们刚才提到天宫图这个概念，天空图的英文应该是Horoscope
We just mentioned the concept of the Horoscope. The English for “天宫图” should be Horoscope. (Horoscope in Chinese is “天宫图”.)

This word likely originates from the Greek word ὡροσκόπος (horoskopos). Meaning “Hour-marker”.

If you know astrology, you should know this refers to the Ascendant.

In Latin, it’s Horoscopium.

This Greek word Ωροσκόπιο (oroskopio) is Modern Greek.

Of course, learning more languages is actually very important.

For studying and researching astrology, using English is probably the simplest. Followed by Spanish and Hindi.

其它常用的语言主要有古希腊语、阿拉伯语、拉丁语、梵语，因为很多古代文献都是这些语言写的
Other commonly used languages mainly include Ancient Greek, Arabic, Latin and Sanskrit. Because many ancient texts are written in these languages.

这些语言其实都不太容易学习
These languages are actually not very easy to learn.

I will recommend some excellent books later. Most of them are in English.

The chart we see here is actually the birth chart of Mr. Bruce Lee.

I will use this chart later to introduce some basic concepts.

Finally, under the Chinese “天宫图” (Horoscope), I’ve written its Sanskrit term कुण्डली (kundali)

It means “circle” or “ring”. This word has other meanings too.

There’s no need to emphasize that astrology is Western.

天宫图占星学在中国也流行过一段时间，大概应该是隋唐时期传入的中国并兴起的
Horoscopic Astrology was also popular in China for a period. It was probably introduced and became popular around the Sui and Tang dynasties.

一般被称作七政四余，它也有其它名字
It was generally called “七政四余”（Qizheng SiYu, Seven Planets and Four Auxiliary Stars）. It has other names.

The term “Western Astrology” in many contexts has specifically come to mean Horoscopic Astrology.

But the Horoscopic Astrology is not entirely so-called “Western”. It also includes Egyptian, Sassanid Persian, Arabic, and Indian traditions.

这一点我们会在介绍占星学历史的部分来详细介绍
We will cover this in detail in the part about the history of astrology.

重要的是，你对于「西方」 Western 这个词的定义是什么
What’s important is what your definition of “Western” is.

我们在日常对话中谈论的西方世界，主要指的是西欧和北美，还有澳洲和新西兰，有可能还包括南美，但很少会包含东欧
In everyday conversation, when we talk about the “Western world”, it mainly refers to Western Europe and North America. And Australia and New Zealand. Possibly also South America. But it rarely includes Eastern Europe.

人们在谈论西方文明时，通常也不会包含埃及、波斯和阿拉伯
When people talk about Western civilization, they usually don’t include Egypt, Persia, and Arabia.

These are the four main branches of astrology.

The first is Mundane Astrology, which is about predicting world events.

Because Mundane Astrology is sometimes translated as “经世占星学” (Worldly Astrology) in Chinese. The name might be taken from the “皇极经世书” (Book of Supreme World Ordering Principles) by Shao Yong, using the term “经世”.

相当于经世之学，但不完全都是
It’s akin to statecraft, but not entirely.

这是占星学最早的一个应用领域了，它是用来预测国家和地区层面的事件
This is one of the earliest application fields of astrology. It’s used to predict events at the national and regional level.

最早是根据天象预兆，后来是根据星盘来做预测
Initially based on celestial omens, later based on charts for predictions.

我个人是不推荐大家一开始就研究这个层面的内容
I personally don’t recommend starting with this area.

先记住这个关键词：预测世界事件
Just remember this keyword: Predicting world events.

第二个主要分支是本命占星学，主要就是用来去看出生星盘
The second main branch is Natal Astrology. It’s mainly used for interpreting birth charts.

这个应该是目前最广泛流行的领域了，很多占星学的学生最先接触的就是这个分支
This is probably the most widely popular field now. Many astrology students first encounter this branch.

本命的是研究出生星盘
Natal astrology studies the birth chart.

本命的梵语应该是जन्मन् （Janman）
The Sanskrit for Natal is जन्मन् (Janman).

出生星盘也被称作本命盘 （Natal Chal）
The birth chart is also called the Natal Chart.

你可以简单的理解成「算命」，因为出生星盘的确是需要计算得到的
You can simply understand it as “算命” (calculating natal, fortune-telling in Chinese). Because the birth chart indeed needs to be calculated.

所以「算命」这个说法应该是没有错的，就是「计算本命盘」
So the term “算命” isn’t wrong in this sense. It means “calculating the natal chart”.

「算命」 「计算本命盘」
“Fortune-telling”(calculating natal) -> “Calculating the natal chart”.

当然在命理学中会有这个所谓的「命运」这个说法
Of course, in fate calculation, there’s the concept of “destiny” and “luck”.

「命」和「运」其实是两个不同的概念
“Destiny” and “Luck” are actually two different concepts.

在生物学里面讲的有一句话叫，「基因和环境决定性状」
In biology, there’s a saying: “Genes and environment determine traits.”

基因是先天的固定的
Genes are innate and fixed.

我们的出生时间地点和家庭不是我们自己所决定的，这就相当于所谓的「命」
Our birth time, location, and family are not decided by ourselves. This is equivalent to so-called “Destiny”.

不同的时间段可能会发生不同的状况，这个相当于「运」
Different time periods may bring different circumstances. This is equivalent to “Luck”.

所以要注意分清「算命」和「推运」，这是两个概念
So, pay attention to distinguish “Fortune-telling” (calculating destiny) and “Predicting Luck”, they are two concepts.

至于说我们的行动是否是由我们自己决定的，这是本命占星学背后的哲学问题
As for whether our actions are determined by ourselves, this is a philosophical question behind Natal Astrology.

关于命定与自由意志的一个争论，这个我会在介绍本命占星学时来做一些详细介绍
A debate about determinism and free will. I will cover this in more detail when introducing the Natal Astrology.

开端/择时占星学，它主要的内容是，关于事件星盘和选择吉时
Inceptional/Electional astrology. Its main content is about event charts and choosing auspicious times.

当然这个部分目前主要被称作是择时占星学，选择好的时间
This part is now mainly called Electional Astrology, Choosing favorable times.

主要比如说 嫁娶、开张或者出行
Mainly for things like marriage, opening a business, or travel.

择时占星学在中国被称作为 「弧角天星择日」
In China, Electional Astrology is called “弧角天星择日”(Arc-Altitude Astrological Date Selection).

它可能更强调的是日期的概念
It might emphasize the concept of the date more.

择时占星学在梵语里面是मुहूर्त (Muhurta)，这个词也表示一天的三十分之一，就是大概48分钟
The Sanskrit for Electional Astrology is मुहूर्त (Muhurta). This word also means one-thirtieth of a day, about 48 minutes.

这个分支学说最开始的主要概念应该是开端占星学，它类似于这个世俗占星学对于世界事件的一个分析，但它的概念更小一些
The main concept of this branch initially was probably Inceptional Astrology. It’s similar to Mundane Astrology’s analysis of world events, but its scope is smaller.

人们会认为，某个个人事件的开始的时间，会对这个事件产生一些影响
People believe that the start time of a personal event influences the entire event.

因为事件的开始时间相当于事件的诞生，概念上很像本命占星学
Because the start time of an event is like its birth. Conceptually, it’s very similar to Natal Astrology.

比如说结婚的时间，可能会影响整个婚姻的状况，如果一个好的时间还好，那坏的时间可能会很糟
For example, the time of marriage might affect the entire marital situation. A good time might be fine, but a bad time could be terrible.

后来的人们因为这个观念，将一个好的时间作为开端，来影响整个事件的状况
Later, because of this concept, people used a favorable time as the inception to influence the entire event’s outcome.

一个好的时间对于事件的具体影响有多少，这个是很难评价的
How much specific influence a good time has on an event is difficult to evaluate.

影响并不是绝对的，这背后也有一套它的一个哲学问题
The influence is not absolute. There’s also a set of philosophical questions behind it.

当然了，除了宇宙诞生的这个时刻以外，并不存在一个绝对完美的一个美好时刻
Of course, aside from the moment of the universe’s creation, there is no absolutely perfect, wonderful moment.

第四个主要分支是卜卦占星学，那它的主要的作用就是回答问题
The fourth main branch is Horary Astrology. Its main function is to answer questions.

「卜卦」这个词，原本这个英文应该是 Horary，他被翻译成卜卦完全是意译
The original English for “卜卦”(divination) should be Horary. Translating it as “卜卦”(divination) is purely a semantic translation.

当然这个方法的作用跟中国传统上的卜卦的思路是一致的，都是根据提问时间来解答问题
Of course, the function of this method aligns with the approach of traditional Chinese divination. Both answer questions based on the time the question is asked.

Horary 这个词应该是来源于这个拉丁语的 Hora 小时，对应提出问题的这个时间
The word “Horary” probably comes from the Latin “Hora” meaning hour, corresponding to the time the question is posed.

那 Horary 这个希腊语应该是 ερώτησης （erotesis），阿拉伯语好像是 مسائل (masa’il )
The Greek for Horary should be ερώτησης (erotesis). In Arabic, I think it’s مسائل (masa’il).

这些词的意思其实都是询问和提问
The meaning of these words is essentially “inquiry” and “question”.

梵语是प्रश्न (Prashna) ，这个词意思就是问题
In Sanskrit, it’s प्रश्न (Prashna), meaning “question”.

这些是占星学的主要四个分支，之后我会详细介绍一下每个分支大概是什么
These are the four main branches of astrology. I will give a detailed introduction of each branch later.

如果你之前已经了解占星学的话，你会发现我讲这些内容好像跟大家讲的一样，跟你之前学的也是一样的
If you were already familiar with astrology before, you might find that what I’m saying seems similar to what others say, and similar to what you learned before.

那接下来我会讲一下就是，重新定义占星学，把占星学定义为模型
Next, I will talk about redefining the astrology. Defining astrology as a model.

我不知道你对于这个模型的概念是怎么理解的，我个人是觉得用模型这个词来解释是比较恰当的
I don’t know how you understand the concept of a model. Personally, I find using the term “model” to explain it quite appropriate.

你可以理解成数学模型或者是机器学习模型
You can think of it as a mathematical model or a machine learning model.

因为我的专业背景是计算机科学，做过几年的算法工程师，也搞过很多的机器学习模型，因为这些模型很多都是用来做预测的
Because my professional background is in computer science. I worked as an algorithm engineer for several years. And I’ve worked on many machine learning models. Many of these models are used for prediction.

我也发现预测这个概念，机器学习模型的预测，跟占星学里面的解读是很相似的，所以拿过来做一下类比
I also found the concept of prediction in machine learning models is very similar to interpretation in astrology. So I’m drawing an analogy.

话题突然这个跳转到理工科内容是有点奇怪
Suddenly jumping to STEM topics might seem strange.

不过不用担心这些，这里面这个数学公式都是很容易理解的
But don’t worry about that. The mathematical formulas here are easy to understand.

我们可以先看这个左边部分
Let’s first look at the left part.

如果我们将这个真实世界简化成一个数学公式的话，那么这个函数应该就直接对应一个「绝对真理」
If we simplify the real world into a mathematical formula, then this function should directly correspond to an “Absolute Truth”.

「绝对真理」会将我们这个问题 x 计算成一个正确答案 y，但问题是我们不知道「绝对真理」是什么
“Absolute Truth” would compute our question x into a correct answer y. But the problem is, we don’t know what “Absolute Truth” is.

所以尝试构建一个能够模拟真实世界的预测模型，预测模型是一个「相对真理」，它是估计的
So we try to build a predictive model that can simulate the real world. The predictive model is a “Relative Truth”. It’s an estimated hypothesis.

所以这个「相对真理」会将我们的问题 x ，可能的正确答案 y 帽
So this “Relative Truth” takes our question x to a possible correct answer y-hat.

我们最终的目的是得到真实世界的「绝对真理」，而过程是根据不断迭代预测模型的「相对真理」而尽可能的接近于「绝对真理」
Our ultimate goal is to obtain the “Absolute Truth” of the real world. And the process involves iterating the predictive model’s “Relative Truth” to get as close as possible to “Absolute Truth”.

至于说这个预测模型的「相对真理」是什么，这个取决于你如何构建这个预测模型
As for what the “Relative Truth” of the predictive model is, that depends on how you construct this predictive model.

如果你理解了这个概念，我们再看右边
If you understand this concept, let’s look at the right side.

作为模型的占星学，尝试构建一种「相对真理」来解答世俗问题
Astrology as a model attempts to construct a “Relative Truth” to answer worldly questions.

占星学的目的也是期望能够尽可能的接近「真实世界」
Astrology’s purpose is also to hopefully get as close as possible to the “real world”.

根据给定的时间和地点来绘制星盘，然后根据星盘进行解读和预测

回溯性理解是，不以简单的对与错来判断历史和现有的知识体系，而是将其视为其产生的特定历史、技术和社会语境下的有效模型，理解其智慧、当时的有效性与价值，以及其不可避免的时代局限性，从中汲取营养，而非简单地弃之脑后
Retrospective Understanding is: not judging historical and existing knowledge systems simply as right or wrong, but viewing them as valid models within their specific historical, technological, and social contexts. Understand their wisdom, their validity, and value at the time, and their inevitable historical limitations. Draw nourishment from them, rather than simply discarding them.

In our current era, we should be able to look back at the past, equivalent to returning to that era, and re-understand the astrology of that time under its conditions.

Present-Tense Building takes pragmatism as its guiding principle. Focus on solving present problems.

技术或方法的价值不应该取决于新、旧或意识形态的纯粹性，而是取决于它在当下解决问题的有效性
The value of a technique or method should not depend on its novelty, age, or ideological purity. But rather on its effectiveness in solving problems in the present.

知识和技术的发展是一个无限的、非线性的一个迭代过程
The development of knowledge and technology is an infinite, non-linear iterative process.

当前的一个最佳方法应该被积极使用，拥抱反馈和变化
The current best method should be actively used. Embrace feedback and change.

将被超越，视为成功的自然结果
View being surpassed as a natural outcome of success.

为未来的修正、升级乃至颠覆，预留开放空间

Although astrology has iterated for over 2000 years, there is still much content worth perfecting.
